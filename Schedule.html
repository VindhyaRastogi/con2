Minute to Minute Program of the 14th Convocation of IIITD
Saturday, 1st November, 2025
10.45 a.m. Arrival of the Hon’ble Lt. Governor of Delhi and Chancellor, IIIT Delhi
10.50 a.m. Robing ceremony (Green Room, Convocation Hall)
11:00 a.m. Welcome note
11:01 a.m. Academic Procession
11:05 a.m. National Anthem
11:07 a.m. Convocation Song
11:13 a.m. Convocation is declared open by the Hon'ble Chancellor
11:14 a.m. Director’s Report and Address
11:30 a.m. Address by the Chairman, Board of Governors, and Introduction of the Chief Guest
11:40 a.m. Convocation Address by the Chief Guest
12:00 p.m. Address by Hon'ble Chancellor
12:10 p.m. Presentation of Chancellor’s Gold Medal by the Hon’ble Chancellor
Presentation of various Medals by the Hon’ble Chancellor and Chief Guest
Presentation of Doctoral Dissertation Award by the Hon’ble Chancellor and Chief Guest
12:28 p.m. Announcement of Honors students
12:30 p.m. Award of Ph.D. Degrees in the Convocation Hall
12:45 p.m. Chairman, Board of Governors, signs the scroll of all the Award of Degrees
12:47 p.m. Oath-taking by all Degree Recipients
12:52 p.m. Director requests the Chairman, Board of Governors to declare the Convocation closed
12:53 p.m. Convocation is declared closed by the Chairman, Board of Governors
12:55 p.m. National Anthem
1:00 p.m. M.Tech. & B.Tech. students will move to their respective halls for the degree distribution, followed by lunch
LUNCH
